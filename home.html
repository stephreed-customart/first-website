Book It!

You can buy books anywhere, but we want to make Book it! the best place to buy books. Not only will the app learn and recommend books you may like, you can also keep track of what you read and what you want to read. Each user will have their own library. This app will have a very substantial filtering search page and have popular books, bestsellers, etc. sections. Users will also be able to like authors or a book series and receive alerts when new books are available.
